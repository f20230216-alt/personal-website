# Personal Website – Brand Guidelines

# 1. Brand Mission

Represent the work of someone who builds thoughtful digital products from concept to production.

The website should inspire confidence rather than impress with complexity.

---

# 2. Brand Position

I am not presenting myself as:

- A student
- A Flutter developer
- A product manager
- A founder

Instead, I am positioning myself as:

A Product Builder.

Someone who combines product thinking, engineering, user experience, and execution.

---

# 3. Brand Personality

The website should feel:

Calm

Confident

Thoughtful

Curious

Premium

Reliable

Intentional

Never:

Loud

Overly playful

Corporate

Aggressive

Trendy

Overdesigned

---

# 4. Emotional Response

Visitors should feel:

"I trust this person."

"This person cares about quality."

"This person thinks before building."

"I'd like to work with this person."

---

# 5. Communication Style

Professional.

Warm.

Direct.

Clear.

Evidence-based.

Avoid:

Buzzwords

Marketing language

Exaggeration

Clickbait

---

# 6. Brand Principles

Show work.

Show process.

Show thinking.

Show growth.

Never exaggerate.

Never overpromise.

Always prioritize authenticity.

---

# 7. Visual Personality

Large typography.

Generous whitespace.

Minimal color palette.

Subtle gradients.

Premium photography only when necessary.

Screenshots over illustrations.

Real work over decoration.

---

# 8. Long-Term Evolution

The brand should naturally grow to include:

Products

Writing

Case Studies

Talks

Open Source

Without changing its identity.
